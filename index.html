What to Do When You Need Emergency Towing

Knowing how to respond in an emergency can help make the situation less stressful. Here are some steps to take if you find yourself in need of emergency towing services:

  1. Stay Calm: First and foremost, try to stay calm and collected. Being prepared can help reduce anxiety and ensure that you make the right decisions.
  2. Ensure Your Safety: If possible, move your vehicle to a safe location, such as the shoulder of the road. Turn on your hazard lights to alert other drivers.
  3. Call for Help: Contact a reputable towing company like Javi’s Towing And Transport Orlando. We’ll ask for the necessary details, including your location and the nature of the emergency, so we can send the right help your way.
  4. Wait Safely: While waiting for assistance, remain in your vehicle if you’re on a busy road, or exit the vehicle if it’s safer. It’s best to wait in a location where you’re not exposed to potential danger.
  5. Provide Information: When our team arrives, provide them with all relevant information, including the nature of the emergency and where you need the vehicle towed. Our professional drivers will handle the rest.
